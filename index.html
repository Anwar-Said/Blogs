About

Anwar Said is a Ph.D. research scholar in the Department of Computer Science at Information Technology University, Lahore, Pakistan. He received MPhil (2016) degree in Computer Science from Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad, Pakistan. His research interests are in the area of representation learning, social networks, and machine learning.
