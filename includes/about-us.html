About

I am a graduate student in the department of Computer Science, Information Technology University, Lahore, Pakistan (Fall 2017-Present). Previously, I completed my MS from Quaid-i-Azam University, and worked as a programmer and development team lead at Project Vision, Islamabad, Pakistan. My main area of interest is the exciting new field of learning and reasoning with graph-structured data using graph theory and machine learning algorithms.
